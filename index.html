CBCF History

Established in 1986 by a dedicated group of volounteers, The Canadian Breast Cancer Foundation worked collaboratively to fund, support, and advocate for:

  • Relevant and innovative research
  • Meaningful education and awareness programs
  • Early diagnosis and effective treatment
  • A positive quality of life for those living with breast cancer

In 2019 the CBCF merged with the Canadian Cancer Society. More information can be found at cancer.ca
